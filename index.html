Ultrix OS is a Linux distribution made to execute Windows, Mac OS, Android, and Linux Applications. Ultrix OS is made and installed with many kinds of desktop GUI's so users can adjust the interface to fit their needs.
Ultrix OS may be unstable and is not made for all needs and is made by 13 year olds, No this is not a joke. Ultrix OS was made by a 12 year old editing his Linux installation.
Ultrix OS can be installed on Virtual Machines, PC's, Mac's, and other supported devices. A list of software installed by Ultrix OS by default can be found in the Ultrix Documentation.
